Revised Functional Requirements [Review 1] 
Intelligent Canteen Management System

The following changes and enhancements are recommended for the system.
1. Menu Management
1.	The system shall display meal-wise menu items for breakfast, lunch, snacks, and dinner.
2.	The system shall allow administrators to modify menu items on a daily or meal-wise basis.
3.	The system shall assign and display the price of each menu item.
2. Student Wallet and Billing
4.	The student dashboard shall display a prepaid wallet containing the total semester meal balance.
5.	The system shall automatically deduct or refund the corresponding amount based on meal booking confirmation or cancellation.
6.	The system shall display the total cost of booked items and the updated wallet balance after every transaction.
3. Meal Booking and Ordering
7.	Students shall be allowed to book meals up to two days in advance.
8.	The system shall accept meal bookings until 2:00 PM, two days prior to the scheduled meal.
9.	The system shall allow meal cancellations until 4:00 PM, two days prior to the scheduled meal.
10.	Students shall be allowed to order any menu item multiple times without quantity restrictions.
11.	Students shall be allowed to opt for meals up to two hours before serving time, even if they did not book the meal two days earlier.
12.	Students shall be allowed to avail meals from any hostel canteen irrespective of their assigned hostel.
4. QR-Based Meal Verification
13.	The system shall generate a QR code containing the selected menu items for meal verification and delivery purposes.
5. Administrative Monitoring and Analytics
14.	The admin dashboard shall display the total number of plates ordered and the total number of plates delivered.
15.	The system shall generate item-wise analytical graphs and reports to support demand analysis and operational monitoring.
6. Future Scope Considerations
16.	The system may allow students to register and book meals for invited guests. Guest meal charges can be deducted directly from the student’s wallet or processed through separate payment options.
17.	The system may be extended to support faculty and staff members with dedicated login credentials, wallet/payment facilities, and meal booking functionalities.
18.	The system may include provisions for managing special guest invitees during institutional events, conferences, seminars, or official visits. Administrators may generate temporary meal access and QR-based verification for such guests.
